NARUTO!

I am sure you saw this coming, LOL!
Set in the time of the 5 great Ninja nations, Naruto, a native of Konoha (Hidden Leaf Village), a Jinchuriki and an outcast amongst his peers vowed to become Hokage.
With his low aptitude in Ninjitsu, mediocre in Taijutsu, and inability to perform Genjutsu, he embarks on an arduous journey to fulfil his dream.
Set to rescue and preserve his friendship with Sasuke, Naruto makes this personal mission a guiding light towards becoming the Hokage of Konoha.
